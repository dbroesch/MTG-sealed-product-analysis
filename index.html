🚨 This is the trickiest item to sell. Italian Legends packs are so rarely traded that most convention vendors may decline or low-ball heavily due to illiquidity risk. Key factors that affect your price:

• Wrapper condition — any crease, moisture, or fading tanks value
• Provenance — if you have a purchase receipt or original source, show it. Fakes exist.
• Target audience — this sells best to a dedicated vintage collector, not a general MTG player. Find the right buyer at the convention.
• English packs are worth far more (~$800+) — Italian has a smaller collector market

If you can't find a motivated collector at the convention, consider Heritage Auctions or a specialty vintage MTG Discord rather than a vendor buylist.
