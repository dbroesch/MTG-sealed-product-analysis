⚠️ Condition is critical for Revised duals. White-bordered cards show wear more visibly than black-bordered cards — look for play scratches on the white border, back whitening, and corner wear. A card being sold as NM that has visible border wear is at best LP, and LP dual lands trade $80–$120 below NM. Inspect under good lighting. Also confirm it's actually Revised (white border) and not an FBB copy being priced differently.
